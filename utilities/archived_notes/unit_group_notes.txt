Things groups should be able to do

add group to position
add_group;Group1

add group to single position
add_group;Group1;0,9

add group to it's position from a direction off the map
spawn_group;Group1;East;Group1

add group to it's starting position
add_group;Group1;Starting

add group to it's starting position from a direction off the map
spawn_group;Group1;East;Starting

move group to a single position
move_group;Group1;0,9

move group to it's starting position
move_group;Group1;Starting

move group to it's position
move_group;Group1;Group1

move group to another group's positions
move_group;Group1;Group2

remove group
remove_group;Group1
